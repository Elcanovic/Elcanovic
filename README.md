## 🎮 About Me
I am a passionate Game Developer with **3+ years of experience** in Unity, specializing in both 2D and 3D architectures. Beyond standard development, I have a strong background in Godot for 2D pixel-art projects and a deep-seated interest in the intersection of AI and game engine design.

## 🚀 Featured Projects
* **Plug the Unplugged:** Xsolla Game Jam Winner. A puzzle-mechanic title developed in Unity.
* **FPS Robot Shooter:** A 3D shooter focusing on enemy AI behavior and responsive combat mechanics.
* **Royal Rush:** A fast-paced 2D platform runner utilizing procedural movement concepts.

## 🔬 Research & Development
My current R&D work focuses on pushing the boundaries of game engine design through:
* **Agentic Systems:** Researching autonomous NPC decision-making.
* **Spatial Reasoning:** Implementing advanced environmental awareness.
* **Simulative Learning:** Exploring how agents learn within physics-based simulations.
* **Procedural Generation:** Creating algorithmic systems for dynamic level design.
